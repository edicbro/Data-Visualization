# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance
across products, categories, payment modes and sale types.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI | Excel

---

## 📸 Preview
![Dashboard](Sales_Dashboard.png)

---

## ✨ What It Shows
- **KPIs** — Buying Price (333K), Avg Profit (20.58%), Total Profit (68.38K)
- **Monthly Trends** — Revenue & profit across 12 months
- **Top Products** — Product30 leads in profit
- **Payment Split** — Cash (199K) vs Online (202K)
- **Sale Types** — Direct (208K) > Online (134K) > Wholesale (59K)
- **Category Profits** — Category02 tops at 24%

---

## 💡 Key Finding
Direct sales dominate, March-April shows a dip,
and year-end sees the strongest recovery.
